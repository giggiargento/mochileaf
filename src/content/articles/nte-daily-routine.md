A sustainable NTE day: log in, claim mail, run dailies, spend natural resin, touch City Tycoon, log out. Add weekly bosses and events on reset days only.

## Every login

Collect idle rewards and mail. Complete daily commissions. Run one resin activity you need today (ascension books, credits, or weapon mats).

## Weekly reset

Touch weekly bosses and limited shops when you have the energy—skip them on busy weeks without guilt. Refresh anomaly bounties if you are chasing account levels. If you pull on banners, let weekly income set the pace instead of impulse.
