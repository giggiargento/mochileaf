Redeem codes from the in-game menu (Settings → Account → Redeem). Codes expire — treat this page as a reminder to check official social posts, not a live database.

## How to redeem

Copy the code exactly, including caps. One account per code unless stated otherwise. Rewards usually arrive in the mailbox within a few minutes.

## Where new codes appear

Follow the official NTE channels and patch notes. Fan sites aggregate codes quickly; verify on a second source before sharing with friends.
