Our first virtual tea gathering was exactly what we hoped for: soft chat, island screenshots, works-in-progress farms, and a lot of “just one more day” energy shared without pressure.

## What showed up

Players brought ACNH sunset photos, Stardew farm layouts, and NTE teams they were enjoying casually—not optimized rosters, just favorites. Fan art and desk setups reminded us that cozy gaming is as much about **where you sit** as what you play.

## Why we host these

Tea gatherings are Mochileaf’s living room—low stakes, high warmth. If you missed this one, watch the site for the next invite. Bring a screenshot and your drink of choice; stats spreadsheets can stay at the door.
