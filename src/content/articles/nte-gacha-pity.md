Gacha systems can feel louder than the rest of NTE. This overview keeps the vocabulary simple so you can decide **if and when** to pull, not whether you “failed” by waiting.

## Banners in one breath

Limited banners spotlight specific characters for a set window. Standard banners rotate wider pools. Neither one is required to enjoy story chapters or City Tycoon.

## Soft pity and hard pity

Games often raise your odds quietly as pulls add up (soft pity), then guarantee a high rarity after a long dry spell (hard pity). Exact numbers change with patches—check in-game details or official notes rather than trusting outdated charts.

## A cozy approach

- Pull for characters you like aesthetically, not because a list called them mandatory.
- Keep a small emergency currency stash for characters you know you want.
- Remember Chiz and story rewards cover a lot of ground without touching banners.

Spending is optional comfort. Waiting is a valid strategy.
