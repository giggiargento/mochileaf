City Tycoon is NTE's quieter second home—a place to place buildings, watch streets fill in, and earn **Chiz** without touching the gacha banner. If you enjoy daily rituals, this mode may become your favorite menu.

## Why Chiz matters here

Chiz arrives through city progression rather than luck on a limited banner. That makes him a friendly first striker for players who prefer steady work over surprise pulls.

## Grain Market rhythm

Check the Grain Market when you log in. Upgrades do not need to happen in one marathon session—a few minutes several days a week often feels better than a single six-hour push.

## A light weekly habit

1. Collect idle city rewards and mail.
2. Spend city currency on one upgrade you can see finishing soon.
3. Run combat dailies with Chiz once he is on your team.

Stop when it stops feeling cozy. The city will wait for you tomorrow.
