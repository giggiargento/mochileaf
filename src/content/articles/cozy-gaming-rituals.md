A cozy session starts before the title screen. Dim overhead lights, warm the mug, and decide **one small goal** for the night—water crops, finish a bundle, or simply visit villagers—rather than a endless checklist.

## Light and sound

Lower blue-heavy brightness on screens when you can. Gentle instrumental playlists or in-game rain audio often feel calmer than competitive stream overlays.

## Time boundaries

Set a loose end time (“after this in-game day”) so play stays restorative. Saving mid-afternoon in Stardew or after dailies in NTE are natural stopping points.

## Why it matters on Mochileaf

We write guides the same way we play: curated, warm, and respectful of your pace. Rituals are not productivity hacks—they are permission to enjoy games as a quiet corner of the day.
