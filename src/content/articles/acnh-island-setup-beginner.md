Your first days on the island are about unlocking tools, placing infrastructure, and leaving room for later terraforming. Move slowly — paths and rivers are easier to adjust once you have the vaulting pole and ladder.

## First placements

Keep Resident Services near your airport landing for visitors. Leave flat space for villager homes in a loose neighborhood so you can add paths later.

Do not finalize your entire map before you can terraform; sketch zones instead (residential, market, forest, beach hangout).
