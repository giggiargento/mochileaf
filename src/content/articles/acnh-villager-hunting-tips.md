Inviting a specific villager is part luck, part gentle routine. Mystery tours, campsite amiibo, and trading with friends are the three main paths—pick the one that matches your budget and patience.

## Mystery tours

Save Nook Miles tickets for dedicated hunting sessions. Empty a plot or keep an open campsite slot so you can invite someone home immediately.
